## Highlights
- Trans people should not just be included; they should not just be quoted; they should not just be covered; they should be *centered,* made the explicit focus of the piece. Trans people must always be the point of trans coverage, anything else further re-entrenches our marginalization and sends the message that cis people are both more important and better qualified to talk about our lives than we are.
